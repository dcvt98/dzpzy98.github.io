Me and My Portfolio

Hello! I am a Masters Biology student researching chemical and behavioural ecology. During my biology program, I really enjoyed my biostatistics courses where I was taught how to code. It was then I realized the myriad applications of data in our world and it was then I developed my interest in understanding the stories that data can tell us. At school, the ability to work with data was key when I started to participate in biological research. But, I was surprised that I was able to apply the skills I developed in my classes in my extra-curricular interests working as a Game Video Analytics Coach helping my teammates at my university's rugby team while nursing an injury. Our collective efforts paid off that season when we went undefeated in provincial league play and qualified for Canada's first ever men's university national championship. From then, I have worked to keep my programming skills sharp by taking part in data science competitions on kaggle.com and furthering my knowledge with bootcamps.
